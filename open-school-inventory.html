About

Open School Inventory System — Single HTML file, no server required. All data stored in browser localStorage. Export backups regularly — clearing browser data or switching browsers loses data without a backup.

Students browse to your server IP on their phones, open this page, and use the Check Out tab. Camera QR scanning requires HTTPS or localhost. For plain http:// on your local network, manual item search still works.
